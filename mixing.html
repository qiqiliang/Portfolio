Mixing with water

Have you ever heard of like dissolves like? Well, this saying is just right for mixing with water! Water is a polar substance, which means that on one end of the water molecule, it is slightly negative and on the other end of the molecule, it is slightly positive. Water can only dissolve other polar substances. Any non polar substance, such as oil will not be able to dissolve in water.

Materials

  • Apple juice
  • Oil(Cooking)
  • Milk
  • Shampoo
  • Laundry detergent
  • Maple Syrup
  • Rubbing alcohol
  • Jars for each of the liquids
  • water
  • stirring rod

Procedure

  1. Place the same amount water in each of the jars.
  2. Add one tablespoon of each liquid to each jar.(All jars should contain a different type of liquid)
  3. Stir the mixture.(Clean the stirring rod between mixing different solutions)
  4. Observe results.
