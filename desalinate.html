Desalinate sea water

The purpose of the experiment is the demonstate how salty water from the oceans evaporate. When the vapor condenses and falls back to Earth, it is once again fresh water.

Materials

  • small bowl
  • small cup
  • plastic wrap
  • water
  • table salt
  • rock/small weight
  • sun

Procedure

  1. In the cup, mix 1 teaspoon of salt with some water.(Water should be about 1 inch from bottom of cup)
  2. Pour the mixture into a bowl.
  3. Place the cup in the middle of the bowl and cover bowl with plastic wrap.
  4. Place a small weight/rock directly on top of the cup and let sit in sunlight for a few hours. Observe your results.
