Creating a water filter

Materials

  • empty, clean soda bottle
  • cotton, gravel, sand for filtering layers
    • dirty water

Procedure

  1. Cut the bottle in half and place the top part over the bottom part. The top will act as the filter while the bottom will collect the filtered water.
  2. Use the filtering materials(sand, gravel, cotton) and create separate filtering layers in top part of bottle.
  3. Pour water on your filter and observe what happens.
