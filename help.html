We are glad you are here! Let's get started on ways you can help your community better conserve water!

  1. Turn off the tap when you are brushing your teeth. Believe it or not, this simple act can save you up to 8 gallons of water per day.
  2. Take shorter showers or install a low showerhead that utilizes 2 gallons of water per minute or less.
  3. Water lawns at dawn or in evening vs in the afternoons to prevent the water from evaporating quickly.
  4. Fix leaks in the house.
  5. When doing laundry, do a full load.
  6. Install a water collector at the end of your drainpipe to collect excess water for watering plants.
