Hard Water vs Soft Water

Water that contains a high mineral content, specifically calcium and magnesium, is called hard water. When soap is added to hard water, the water will have a difficult time forming bubbles. In other words, hard water does not lather as well as soft water. The Epsom salt in the experiment will mimic the calcium and magnesium ions in hard water.

Materials

  • 2 emmpty bottles
  • Epsom salts
  • Water
  • Dishwashing soap

Procedure

  1. Fill both bottles halfway with water.
  2. In one of the bottles, add 2 teaspoons of Epsom salt.
  3. In both bottles, add a few drops of dishwashing soap and shake. Observe the changes.
